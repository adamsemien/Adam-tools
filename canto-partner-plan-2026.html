Creative Activations

These are the plays that don't show up on a standard event calendar. Niche communities, unconventional channels, and creative formats that get you in front of your ICP before anyone else is there.

🏭
ERP User Group Infiltration
NetSuite · Epicor · Microsoft Dynamics · Q3–Q4
This is the most underrated channel in your toolkit. NetSuite alone powers hundreds of mid-market CPG, food & bev, and manufacturing brands — your exact ICP. These companies have clean inventory data in their ERP and a complete mess of product images, specs, and marketing assets in Google Drive. When their NetSuite implementation wraps up, the asset problem becomes visible. That's the Canto moment, and you can be the person waiting for it.
  • SuiteConnect one-day events: NetSuite runs free one-day regional SuiteConnect events in NYC, Chicago, and SF before SuiteWorld. Low cost, high concentration of mid-market operators. Attend one in Q3 as a test before committing to SuiteWorld in October.
  • Find the NetSuite SIs who serve consumer goods: Companies like Circular Edge, ERP Monk, Paapri specialize in NetSuite for product brands. They hit the same DAM-gap moment repeatedly — product is live on NetSuite, assets still in chaos. Reach out: "When your client asks what to do with their product assets after go-live — that's Canto."
  • NetSuite user community: The Oracle NetSuite Community forums and LinkedIn groups have active discussions about product content challenges. Publish one useful post about "what mid-market brands do with their product assets after ERP implementation." No pitch — just genuine content that positions you as the expert they find when they have the problem.
  • Epicor angle: Epicor serves manufacturing and industrial brands (Trystar's world). You already have a deal there. Ask the Trystar contacts if they know other Epicor users who have the same asset problem — a referral from within the same ERP community is the highest-trust introduction possible.
  • Microsoft Dynamics / Business Central: Heavy in fashion and mid-market retail. If Sitation or Gournay has Dynamics clients, ask them to flag the DAM gap at implementation — same play as NetSuite.

Shopify Plus Merchant Community Play
Organic community presence · Q2 ongoing
There's a private Shopify Plus merchant community — essentially a vetted group of high-volume Shopify operators. Your agency partners (Barrel, Avex, BlueSwitch, Netalico) are active in adjacent communities and attend Shopify meetups. The play here is not direct selling — it's being the person your agency partners name-drop when their Shopify Plus clients ask "what do we do with all our product assets?"
  • Ask each agency partner: "Which of your Shopify Plus clients is currently managing 500+ SKUs in Google Drive or Dropbox?" That question alone surfaces the leads. You don't need to attend a community — you need to brief your partners on what question to ask.
  • Shopify dot.dev Toronto (July) is your community entry point: The side events and parties during dot.dev are where Shopify Plus merchants and agency partners mix. That's the highest-value 48 hours in the Shopify ecosystem annually.
  • Co-author content with a Shopify agency: A blog post or LinkedIn article written jointly with Barrel titled "How CPG brands on Shopify Plus are solving the product content problem" — published to their audience, with Canto as the solution. One piece of content that their 10,000 LinkedIn followers see is worth 6 cold outreach campaigns.
  • Shopify local meetups: Most major cities have monthly Shopify meetups (NYC, LA, Chicago, Austin). These are free, small, and attended by merchants and agency partners. Offer to co-present at one with a local agency partner — not a product demo, a real case study from a CPG brand that solved a real problem.
